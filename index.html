LASA Expo

A student-led technology exposition at our school

February 20th, 2024 in Mr. Shockey’s Room

How can I show off my program? Fill out this form.

How do I attend? Walk right in. No sign-up necessary.

Will there be food? Yes! Pizza and donuts.

Are there prizes? All participants who show off their work will get a participation token. A cash prize will be awarded to the person whose program shows the most promise, as determined by a community vote. The winner will be announced during CS club the week after LASA Expo.
